The rooms in my house

My house is a big house. I love it because it is so spacious. There are particular rooms I love about this house. The first one being my bedroom , I spend almost 9 hours sleeping in there so it has to be my favorite room in the whole house. Another one of my favorite is the bathroom . My bathroom is pure white and thats how I like it so because it shows that I keep it clean at all times!

The kitchen is my third favorite and I like it because that's the where the cooking magic happens. I really like a clean kitchen but it is not all-white like the bathroom, instead I put together neutral colors together and it looks really nice. Lastly, the garden . I love being in the garden during spring time because it smells really good and fresh.
